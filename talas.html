***Talas***

Note: Due to the fact that the 2025 calendar is not yet available, the 2024 calendar has been attached.

Prayer for sealing the mouth:

نَوَيتُ أنْ اصُومَ صَوْمَ شَهْرِ رَمَضانَ مِنَ الفَجْرِ إلى المَغْرِبِ، خَلِصَا للهِ تَعَالى، اللهُ أكْبَرُ.

Meaning: I intend to fast the month of Ramadan from dawn to sunset for the sake of Allah Almighty.

Prayer for opening the mouth:

اللَّهُمَّ لَكَ صُمْتُ وَ بِكَ آمَنْتُ وَ عَلَيْكَ تَوَكَّلْتُ وَ على رِزْقِكَ اَفْطَرْتُ فاغْفِرْ لِي ما قَدَّمْتُ وَ ما اَخَّرْتُ

Meaning: O Allah, I truly believe in You, I rely on You, I break my fast with the sustenance You have given me. Forgive me for my mistakes and sins.
