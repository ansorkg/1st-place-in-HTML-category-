```Fitr Sadaka```

During Ramadan, every Muslim is required to give a certain amount of food as a compulsory charity. The best time to give it is before the Eid prayer. You can start giving 1-2 days in advance.

Zadak al-Fitr is given as 1 sou' of food. You can give it from any type of food that is considered food in the community. For example: rice, dates, flour, etc.

In the past, “Sou'” meant a container for measuring grains such as wheat and barley, as well as a container for measuring loose substances. The amount of wheat that fits in that container is the amount of wheat that is given as zadak al-Fitr; the amount of dates that fit in that container is the amount of dates that are given, etc.
