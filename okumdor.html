`````Rules of fasting:`````

What breaks the fast?

  • Eating or drinking something intentionally
  • Having sexual intercourse with a man
  • Intentional vomiting
  • Menstruation (haiz) in women
  • Postpartum bleeding (nifas) in women
  • Intentional ejaculation – istinmaa (masturbation)

What does not break the fast?

  • Rinsing the mouth and nose during ablution.
  • Involuntary ejaculation during the day (ikhtilam).
  • Kissing and hugging one's wife (but if there is a risk of getting aroused and having sex with her, then kissing and hugging is not allowed).
  • When necessary, carefully chewing the child's food and tasting the food.
  • Using perfume or cream.
  • Being in a state of junub while covering the mouth (hadith of Aisha).
