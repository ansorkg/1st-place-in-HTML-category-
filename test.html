"Kitep-Nur"
Republican book reading competition

The Religious Administration of Muslims of Kyrgyzstan and the Islamic University of Kyrgyzstan announce a republican book reading competition called “KITEP – BILIM, KITEP – TARYH, KITEP – NUR”. The competition will be held in three directions for four books.

“Syngan Kylych” and "Adam adebi" “Bakyttyn uronu” and “Adam adebi” “Sahabalar salgan chyiyr jol” and “Adam adebi” To participate in the competition, you must choose one of the three directions and register. Registration will be open until March 31, 2025. On April 12, 2025, an offline competition will be held throughout the Republic.

The grand prize in each direction is a KIA K5 car! In addition, thirteen winners from 2nd to 13th place in each direction will receive Umrah travel tickets, as well as many other attractive gifts!
